<p>In this guide, you will learn how to create and run an influencer marketing campaign from A to Z using inBeat.</p><p>I will be going through:</p><ol><li>Creating an account</li><li>Creating a campaign</li><li>Searching and filtering influencers</li><li>Exporting a campaign</li><li>Contacting your influencers</li></ol><h2 id="steps-to-using-inbeat"><strong>Steps to Using inBeat</strong></h2><h3 id="1-create-an-account"><strong>1. Create an Account</strong></h3><p><em>If you already have an account, you can skip this step.</em></p><p>Go to <a href="https://www.inbeat.co/">inbeat.co</a> and click “Try inBeat for Free”. Once you have signed up, <strong>confirm your email</strong> and login to the platform.</p><h3 id="2-create-a-campaign"><strong>2. Create a campaign</strong></h3><p>The first thing to do is to create a campaign:</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.inbeat.co/img/1.jpg" class="kg-image"></figure><p>Within a campaign, you will be able to add, manage and export your influencers.</p><h3 id="3-select-all-relevant-search-filters"><strong>3. Select all relevant search filters</strong></h3><p>Within the campaign creation process, you will be asked to specify your search:</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.inbeat.co/img/2.jpg" class="kg-image"></figure><p>You can narrow your search to find the ideal influencers for your campaign. <strong>You can modify your search filters later to narrow or broaden your search.</strong></p><h3 id="4-add-influencers-to-your-campaign"><strong>4. Add influencers to your campaign</strong></h3><p>The campaign structure makes influencer marketing easier. You can access your selected influencers at any time by clicking on “Manage Selected”.</p><p>Click on the bookmark button available on each influencer card to add a profile to your campaign:</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.inbeat.co/img/3.jpg" class="kg-image"></figure><h3 id="5-exporting-your-influencers"><strong>5. Exporting your influencers</strong></h3><p>Once you are done adding influencers to a campaign, you can click on the “export list” button. This allows you to export all influencers with their relevant contact information.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.inbeat.co/img/4.jpg" class="kg-image"></figure><h3 id="5-pitching-your-influencers"><strong>5. Pitching your influencers</strong></h3><p>Congratulations, you know have a list of influencers to work with.</p><p>When you export your campaign, you will receive a CSV file, which you can add to your favorite CRM tool. We propose using <a href="https://mailshake.com/">Mailshake</a> or <a href="https://www.gmass.co/">Gmass</a>, but you can use any tool you wish. These tools make pitching influencers much easier to manage.</p>
