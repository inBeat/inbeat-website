Important update to our services

The database & some free tools are discontinued.

Certain features of our platform were not in compliance with third-party Terms & Conditions. As a result, we've decided to immediately shut down the tools and database portions of inBeat that utilize or index data from those sources.

This was not an easy decision, but it's necessary to ensure we remain fully compliant. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding during this transition. We'll continue providing our free toolkit for agencies and focusing on resources that don't rely on those external data sources.
